About Us

The Vital Three Initiative is a student-led health initiative focused on the connection between the mind, skin, and heart.

We believe health should not be viewed as separate systems. Stress, sleep, hormones, emotions, and lifestyle can affect the body in connected ways.

Through education, awareness, and student-led research, we aim to make health knowledge clearer, more accessible, and more meaningful for students.
