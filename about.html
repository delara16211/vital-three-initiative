About Us

The Vital Three Initiative is a student-led health project focused on the connection between the brain, skin, and heart.

Our goal is to make health education clear, modern, and accessible by showing how stress, sleep, lifestyle, and habits can affect multiple systems in the body at once.

Through educational content, student-led research, awareness campaigns, and future collaborations, we aim to help students understand their health in a deeper and more connected way.
