About Us

The Vital Three Initiative is a student-led health initiative focused on the relationship between the brain, skin, and heart.

We believe health should not be understood as separate systems. Stress, sleep, nutrition, hormones, and lifestyle can affect multiple parts of the body at the same time.

Through educational content, research-based articles, awareness campaigns, and future student projects, we aim to make health education more accessible, modern, and impactful.
