About Us

The Vital Three Initiative is a student-led health initiative focused on connecting three key areas of the human body: the brain, the skin, and the heart.

Our goal is to make health education simple, clear, and accessible to students by showing how daily habits such as stress, sleep, nutrition, and lifestyle affect multiple systems at once.

Instead of learning about health in isolated topics, we explore how cardiology, neurology, and dermatology are interconnected, helping students understand their bodies in a more complete and realistic way.

Through content creation, research-based posts, and awareness campaigns, we aim to build a generation that is more informed about their health and how to take care of it.
