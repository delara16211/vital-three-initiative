Our Mission

The Vital Three Initiative aims to simplify health education by connecting the brain, skin, and heart. Through research, content creation, and awareness, we help students understand how their daily habits impact their overall health.

Our Three Pillars

Cardiology

Understanding heart health, circulation, lifestyle habits, and cardiovascular risks.

Neurology

Exploring brain function, stress, sleep, focus, and emotional health.

Dermatology

Analyzing skin health, hormones, acne, and the link between internal and external health.
