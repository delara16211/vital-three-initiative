A student-led health initiative exploring how neurology, dermatology, and cardiology are connected through lifestyle, stress, sleep, and science.

Join the Initiative

Our Mission

The Vital Three Initiative aims to make health education clear, modern, and accessible by exploring the connection between the brain, skin, and heart. Through education, research, and awareness, we help students understand how daily habits can affect multiple systems in the body.

Our Three Pillars

Neurology

We explore brain health, stress, sleep, focus, emotions, and how the nervous system affects the body.

Dermatology

We discuss skin health, acne, hormones, skin science, and how internal health can affect the skin.

Cardiology

We focus on heart health, circulation, exercise, stress responses, and cardiovascular wellness.

What We Do

We create science-based content, publish articles, build awareness campaigns, and aim to conduct student-led research about the connections between health, lifestyle, and the body.

Our blog expands on Instagram posts with deeper explanations, research insights, and student-friendly articles.
