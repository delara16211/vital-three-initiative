Brain. Skin. Heart.

A student-led health initiative exploring how the mind, skin, and heart are connected through stress, sleep, lifestyle, and science.

Join the Initiative

Our Mission

Our mission is to make health education clear, modern, and accessible by helping students understand how the brain, skin, and heart work together as connected systems. Through awareness, research, and educational content, The Vital Three Initiative aims to show how daily habits can affect overall health.

The Vital Three Pillars

Mind

We explore brain health, stress, sleep, focus, emotions, and how the nervous system influences the body.

Skin

We discuss skin health, acne, hormones, skincare science, and how internal health can affect the skin.

Heart

We focus on heart health, circulation, exercise, stress responses, and cardiovascular wellness.

What We Do

We create science-based content, publish articles, raise awareness, and build student-led projects that explain the connection between lifestyle and health.
