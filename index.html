Brain. Skin. Heart.

A student-led initiative exploring how cardiology, neurology, and dermatology are deeply connected through lifestyle, stress, sleep, and science.

Join the Initiative

Our Mission

The Vital Three Initiative aims to simplify health education by connecting the brain, skin, and heart. Through research, content creation, and awareness, we help students understand how daily habits impact overall health.

Our Three Pillars

Cardiology

Understanding heart health, circulation, lifestyle habits, and cardiovascular wellness.

Neurology

Exploring brain function, stress, sleep, focus, and emotional health.

Dermatology

Analyzing skin health, hormones, acne, and the connection between internal and external health.
